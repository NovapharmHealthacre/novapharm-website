# Pharmaceutical Digital Category Benchmark

**Reviewed:** 14 July 2026  
**Purpose:** identify category expectations and NovaPharm differentiation opportunities without copying wording, layouts or assets.

## Official sites reviewed

- AstraZeneca global corporate website — https://www.astrazeneca.com/
- GSK global/UK corporate website — https://www.gsk.com/en-gb/
- Lonza corporate/CDMO website — https://www.lonza.com/
- DHL Life Sciences & Healthcare logistics website — https://www.dhl.com/global-en/home/industry-sectors/life-sciences-and-healthcare.html

## Observed category patterns

### AstraZeneca

- Uses science and impact stories as the dominant discovery layer rather than a catalogue-first homepage.
- Maintains deep, clearly separated paths for R&D, therapy areas, company, investors, media, sustainability and partnering.
- Makes authorship/review timing and archive warnings visible where information may age or be clinically sensitive.
- Provides obvious third-party link notices and a broad corporate resource architecture.

**NovaPharm implication:** lead with original operating frameworks and partner value, while keeping regulatory status and archive/update limitations visible. NovaPharm should not imitate therapy-area or medicine structures because it is not presenting an approved product catalogue.

### GSK

- Separates company, innovation, products, responsibility, investors, media and careers into distinct, authoritative hubs.
- Gives named scientists and leadership dedicated routes.
- Uses an editorial magazine to translate expertise into accessible stories.
- Separates global corporate information from healthcare-professional and local-market destinations.

**NovaPharm implication:** strengthen canonical leadership, Insights authorship, topical hubs and audience routes without creating HCP or country sites before substantive content exists.

### Lonza

- Organises a complex CDMO offer through a detailed capability hierarchy and knowledge centre.
- Uses precise service language that maps to buyer stage and technical need.
- Avoids forcing every user into one generic contact route.

**NovaPharm implication:** service pages should connect audience intent, lifecycle stage, evidence required and a contextual CTA. NovaPharm’s differentiation should be the integration of market entry, quality, sourcing and commercial viability rather than pretending to offer Lonza-scale manufacturing services.

### DHL Life Sciences & Healthcare

- Frames logistics around sector-specific needs, risk, controlled handling and global execution.
- Uses real operational imagery and solution pathways rather than decorative illustrations alone.
- Connects industry context to practical services and contact routes.

**NovaPharm implication:** traceability, outsourced logistics oversight and batch/document integrity should be explained through realistic evidence flows and qualified partner pathways, without implying NovaPharm owns logistics facilities.

## Category expectations for NovaPharm

1. One clear corporate entity and legal identity.
2. Canonical leadership profiles with stable roles and portraits.
3. Visible distinction between corporate information, professional analysis and regulated/product information.
4. Deep but understandable service and partner architecture.
5. Original insight rather than generic SEO articles.
6. Strong update, source, corrections and scope controls.
7. Realistic media with clear provenance and no false facility ownership.
8. Contextual CTAs based on stakeholder intent.
9. Performance and accessibility despite rich visual presentation.
10. Search and AI discovery that excludes private systems.

## Differentiation opportunity

NovaPharm cannot credibly compete on multinational scale, pipeline breadth or owned infrastructure. It can differentiate through:

- a clear regulatory-commercial operating model;
- practical UK market-entry and product-viability frameworks;
- transparent pre-operational status;
- sourcing-resilience thinking;
- CMO/CDMO and supplier qualification expertise;
- governed batch, documentation and traceability principles;
- founder-led, evidence-based analysis linked to named reviewers.

## Anti-copy controls

- No competitor wording, photography, icons or interaction patterns are copied.
- No competitor scale or operational capability is implied.
- Category observations guide information architecture and trust standards only.
- The benchmark should be refreshed annually and after a major redesign.
