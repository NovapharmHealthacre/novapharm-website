Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical procurement timeline?

Standard RFQ response: 24 business hours. Urgent supply situations: 2-hour response. Full PLPI licensing: 6–12 weeks depending on product complexity.

What documents will you request?

Product specifications (INN, strength, pack size, quantity), current supplier information, pricing benchmarks, volume commitments, and delivery locations. For PLPI licensing, additional regulatory and manufacturing documentation required.

Do you offer cost guarantees?

Cost optimisation ranges typically fall within 8–25% depending on product mix, volumes, and existing contracts. This is an estimate only. Verified analysis with specific figures provided after detailed audit of your data.

How do you verify supplier credentials?

We conduct third-party audits of GMP and GDP compliance, review Quality Certificates, verify manufacturing records, and maintain ongoing audit trails. Full audit reports available to qualified customers upon request.

Are you MHRA regulated?

NovaPharm Healthcare Ltd is registered at Companies House (16716501) as a private limited company. MHRA WDA(H) license details pending formal disclosure. Full regulatory status available on request.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary by product and customer type. Contact us with specific requirements for customized pricing and service terms.
