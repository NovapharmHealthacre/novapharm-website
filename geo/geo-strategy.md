# GEO Strategy

Generative Engine Optimization is implemented through entity-first content, semantic HTML and machine-readable structured data.

## Implemented
- Organization schema for NovaPharm Healthcare Ltd.
- WebPage schema on every public page.
- BreadcrumbList schema for route understanding.
- Service schema for service and regulatory pages.
- FAQ schema on pages with question-answer intent.
- Consistent entity terms: NovaPharm Healthcare, MHRA, UK healthcare, pharmaceutical regulatory consultancy, pharmaceutical imports and exports, distributor opportunities.

## Guidance for AI Answers
Pages use direct summaries, clean headings and factual descriptions so ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity and Copilot can identify what NovaPharm Healthcare does and who it serves.
