Three-pillar sourcing

Three routes. One governed supply strategy.

The proposed model is designed to diversify regulatory, geographic and supplier dependencies without lowering the evidence required for each product.

01Partner development

Direct GMP partnerships

Direct relationships with qualified pharmaceutical manufacturers, particularly MHRA/EU-GMP-aligned partners in India, supported by technical assessment, vendor qualification and quality agreements.

02Regulatory preparation

Product-specific PLPI pathways

Assessment of appropriate EEA-authorised medicines for UK parallel-import pathways, with product-specific applications, supplier evidence and post-authorisation obligations subject to MHRA approval.

03Network development

European buying network

A diversified network of qualified manufacturers, authorised distributors and licensed wholesalers across Germany, the Netherlands, Poland and Romania.

Regulatory status

NovaPharm is pre-operational for regulated wholesale supply. The company will not commence regulated wholesale activities until the required MHRA authorisations and other applicable permissions are in place. Product-specific parallel-import activity remains subject to the grant and maintenance of the relevant PLPI licence.
