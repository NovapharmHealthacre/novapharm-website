## Data not invited

The public website does not invite patient-identifiable data, adverse-event reports, urgent medical details, patient orders or children's data. Contact and upload notices state this boundary. A controlled pharmacovigilance and quality route must be approved before receiving safety data.

## International transfers

The planned hosting, Microsoft and email configurations may involve processing outside the UK. Before activation, the system owner must record the recipient, countries, subprocessor chain, UK adequacy position and, where required, the UK IDTA or Addendum and transfer risk assessment.

Official basis: [ICO right to be informed](https://ico.org.uk/for-organisations/uk-gdpr-guidance-and-resources/individual-rights/individual-rights/right-to-be-informed/), [ICO international transfers](https://ico.org.uk/for-organisations/uk-gdpr-guidance-and-resources/international-transfers/), and [ICO DUAA 2025 overview](https://ico.org.uk/about-the-ico/what-we-do/legislation-we-cover/data-use-and-access-act-2025/the-data-use-and-access-act-2025-what-does-it-mean-for-organisations/).
