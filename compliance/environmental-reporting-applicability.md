# SECR and Carbon Reduction Plan applicability

Assessment date: 11 July 2026  
Conclusion: mandatory SECR reporting not established on verified information; voluntary statement published

Current government guidance describes SECR as mandatory for quoted companies and, subject to the low-energy exemption, unquoted companies and LLPs meeting at least two of: more than 250 employees, turnover over GBP 36 million, and balance sheet over GBP 18 million.

NovaPharm is not presented as quoted. No verified employee, turnover or balance-sheet data available to this implementation establishes that two tests are met. Mandatory SECR reporting therefore cannot be asserted. The accounts owner must reassess company/group status, energy use and any first-year or two-year rules during each annual accounts cycle.

PPN 006 Carbon Reduction Plans concern relevant in-scope central government procurements, generally contracts above GBP 5 million per year including VAT where proportionate and relevant. This is not a universal requirement for every supplier or contract. NovaPharm must assess each opportunity rather than publish a false blanket claim.

Sources: [Sustainability Reporting Guidance 2025-26](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/sustainability-reporting-guidance-2025-26/sustainability-reporting-guidance-2025-26) and [PPN 006 guidance](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/ppn-006-taking-account-of-carbon-reduction-plans-in-the-procurement-of-major-government-contracts).
