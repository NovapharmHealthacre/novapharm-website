# Oncology digital benchmark 2026

Reviewed: 22 July 2026
Purpose: inform NovaPharm's oncology category architecture without copying competitor wording, assets, layouts or claims.

## Benchmark set

| Organisation | What the public experience does well | Limitation for NovaPharm to avoid | NovaPharm design implication |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [AstraZeneca Oncology](https://www.astrazeneca.com/our-therapy-areas/oncology.html) | Strong category navigation, clear scientific platforms, stories, leadership and a visible prescription-medicine information boundary. | Its R&D and medicine claims belong to an established manufacturer and cannot be borrowed. | Use an equally clear scope boundary, but organise around supply continuity and evidence rather than tumour science or medicines. |
| [Roche Oncology](https://www.roche.com/solutions/focus-areas/oncology) | Clear challenge, focus, cases, diagnostics, stories and partnering routes. | Broad treatment and diagnostic claims are not NovaPharm's role. | Lead with the operating challenge, then show a controlled system and engagement model. |
| [Novartis Oncology Research](https://www.novartis.com/research-and-development/research-disease-areas/oncology-research-novartis) | Strong research narrative and programme segmentation. | Pipeline-led language would imply owned programmes and products. | Use programme-formulation categories only as planning lenses, never pipeline or availability claims. |
| [MSD Oncology](https://www.msd.com/research/oncology/) | Disciplined corporate oncology story and useful evidence pathways. | Patient-outcome and discovery language is outside NovaPharm's substantiated position. | Use measured, procurement-facing language with primary-source links. |
| [World Courier clinical logistics](https://www.worldcourier.com/solutions/clinical-trial-logistics) | Converts complexity into specialist logistics decisions and visible programme controls. | Operational claims require actual service delivery evidence. | Show responsibilities, custody evidence, risk points and hand-offs as an architecture, not as achieved service metrics. |
| [World Courier temperature control](https://www.worldcourier.com/expertise/temperature-control-solutions) | Clear connection between temperature range, packaging, monitoring and lane design. | NovaPharm must not imply an owned or active cold chain. | Explain temperature governance as product- and lane-specific planning through qualified partners. |
| [PCI clinical supply](https://pci.com/resources/clinical-supply-chains-support-global-trials/) | Practical supply coordination and risk narrative. | Global delivery scale and facilities are not transferable claims. | Connect development evidence to later market-readiness decisions with explicit accountable owners. |

## Category patterns

1. The strongest sites give the category a clear point of view before listing capabilities.
2. Scientific imagery is most credible when it supports visible evidence or process content.
3. Strong category pages provide pathways into evidence, people and partnership, not only marketing cards.
4. Prescription-medicine and medical-information boundaries are visible, not buried in the footer.
5. High-quality logistics experiences explain custody, condition, time and documentation together.
6. Mature pages distinguish research, commercial, regulatory and patient information contexts.

## NovaPharm opportunity

NovaPharm cannot credibly compete on research scale, marketed medicines, owned facilities or trial history. It can create a distinct and useful category experience around one defensible idea:

> Oncology continuity is designed through evidence, accountable hand-offs and product-specific readiness before a commercial release decision.

This supports four original code-native components:

- Oncology Supply Continuity Architecture
- Formulation & Programme Navigator
- Product Readiness Matrix
- Development-to-Access Continuity Map

These components make the page useful to product owners, manufacturers, CMOs/CDMOs, authorised supply partners and procurement stakeholders without promoting prescription medicines or implying current supply.

## Content guardrails

- B2B corporate and operational content only.
- No patient ordering, treatment, dosage, efficacy or medicine-selection content.
- No product availability, approval, authorisation, customer or partnership claim without evidence.
- No implication that stock photography depicts NovaPharm premises, staff, products or active operations.
- No forecasting or blockchain performance claim; both remain governed roadmaps.
