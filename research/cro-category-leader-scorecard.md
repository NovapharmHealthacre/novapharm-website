# CRO Category-Leader Scorecard

Reviewed: 18 July 2026  
Scope: public CRO and clinical-development web experience  
Method: official-page review plus a 1440 x 900 Chromium snapshot of representative pages. The browser sample is directional, not a complete accessibility or performance audit.

## Decision

NovaPharm should not imitate the scale language of a global full-service CRO. Its strongest defensible position is **evidence architecture for focused clinical-development programmes**: senior programme framing, UK pathway integration, explicit responsibility mapping, controlled documentation, specialist-provider orchestration and continuity into later regulatory and market decisions.

## Browser sample

The 12-page sample recorded no horizontal overflow at 1440 x 900. Approximate declared asset lengths ranged from 1.29 MB to 16.3 MB where responses exposed a content length. Two pages could not be used as complete visual evidence: the selected PPD URL resolved to a generic promotions page and the Charles River browser request returned 403. Their official public content was reviewed separately. Axe snapshots found at least one serious or critical issue on most sampled pages; these snapshots are not conformance audits.

## NovaPharm category-leader criteria

| Dimension | Candidate requirement | Acceptance evidence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Strategic clarity | State exactly what NovaPharm leads, coordinates and does not provide. | Delivery architecture and public status boundary. |
| Sponsor usefulness | Answer who, problem, delivery route and next step in the first viewport. | Hero, context and decision framework. |
| Regulatory responsibility | Preserve sponsor accountability and current UK approval language. | Official-source citations and FAQ. |
| Originality | Build an evidence-architecture proposition, not a reduced global-CRO clone. | Four signature components. |
| Accessibility | Usable lifecycle, service disclosure and navigation without JavaScript. | Semantic HTML, keyboard and Axe acceptance. |
| Performance | Responsive local assets and no third-party runtime dependency. | Performance report and Lighthouse. |
| Credibility | No fabricated scale, sites, laboratories, metrics, testimonials or trial history. | Capability and claims registers. |
