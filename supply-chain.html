Professional Global Supply Chain & Logistics

Enterprise-grade pharmaceutical logistics infrastructure with real-time batch tracking, temperature validation, and pharmacovigilance integration.

Regulatory-First End-to-End Supply Chain Process

1. Supplier Qualification & Audit

Verify EU-GMP certification, conduct on-site audits, confirm GDP compliance, review Quality Certificates and manufacturing records. Timeline: 4–6 weeks

2. Batch Documentation & Certificate of Analysis

Secure Certificate of Analysis (CoA), Quality Overall Summary (QOS), batch traceability records, and full manufacturing documentation. Timeline: 1–2 weeks

3. MHRA PLPI Regulatory Filing

Prepare and submit MHRA PLPI licensing application with packaging verification, Patient Information Leaflet translation, and labelling compliance. Timeline: 6–12 weeks

4. Cold-Chain Logistics & Storage

Temperature-validated storage (2–8°C for biologics), real-time GPS tracking, humidity monitoring, and compliance documentation via qualified logistics partners. Timeline: Ongoing

5. Last-Mile & Customs Clearance

Global distribution with signature-on-delivery tracking, customs brokerage, hazmat certification, and compliance documentation. Timeline: 2–5 business days

6. Pharmacovigilance & Adverse Events Management

Pharmacovigilance: Business-hours monitoring with on-call escalation for urgent adverse events. MHRA Yellow Card integration, adverse event management, batch recall protocols and safety reporting per regulatory requirements. Timeline: Escalation protocols active 24/7
