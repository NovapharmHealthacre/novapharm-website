# Document Libraries

| Library | Primary records | Default retention |
|---|---|---|
| Customers | applications, account documents, contracts, statements | 7 years after closure |
| Suppliers | due diligence, contracts, quality agreements | 7 years after closure |
| Products | product, artwork and technical files | product life plus 10 years |
| Orders | order packs, POs from customers, delivery records | 7 years |
| Invoices | invoices, credit notes and statements | 7 years |
| Purchase Orders | PO, acknowledgements, receipts and matching evidence | 7 years |
| Quality Documents | QMS, complaints, deviations, CAPA and recalls | policy-controlled regulated retention |
| Regulatory Documents | MHRA submissions, licences and correspondence | licence life plus 10 years |
| Warehouse Documents | temperature, receipt, dispatch and reconciliation evidence | 7 years |
| HR Documents | employee files | restricted HR policy |
| Training Records | assignments, evidence and certificates | employment plus policy period |

Folders are provisioned from committed domain events. Users do not create top-level entity folders manually.
