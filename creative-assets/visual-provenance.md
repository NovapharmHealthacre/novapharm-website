The supply-network hero was created on 13 July 2026 with the built-in OpenAI image-generation tool from the approved brief in `creative-assets/image-generation-brief.md`. It depicts no real NovaPharm site, partner, product, vehicle, patient or employee. It is an editorial representation and must not be described as operational evidence.
