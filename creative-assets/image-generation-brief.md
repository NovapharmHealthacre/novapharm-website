# Image Generation Brief

Version: 1.0  
Date: 13 July 2026  
Owner: NovaPharm Healthcare digital programme

## Visual objective

Create a coherent visual language for a compliance-first UK pharmaceutical infrastructure company. Assets should communicate regulated medicine movement, sourcing resilience, quality evidence, batch integrity and connected operations without implying licences, stock, patient outcomes, partnerships or infrastructure ownership.

## Flagship hero prompt

Use case: photorealistic-natural  
Asset type: flagship full-bleed website hero for a UK pharmaceutical company  
Primary request: Create an original, ultra-wide editorial image that expresses a resilient pharmaceutical supply network without showing a generic stock laboratory.  
Scene/backdrop: a bright, precisely controlled pharmaceutical logistics environment combining a clean inspection table, discreet temperature-controlled transport case, unbranded medicine cartons, subtle batch labels, and a layered supply-network visual made from fine physical light lines and translucent map-like panels.  
Subject: regulated medicine movement, batch integrity, cold-chain discipline, and connected sourcing infrastructure.  
Style/medium: premium photoreal editorial photography with restrained scientific 2.5D overlays that look physically integrated, not a collage.  
Composition/framing: 16:9 wide landscape; preserve generous calm negative space across the left 42 percent for white headline copy; place the detailed operational subject mainly on the right and centre-right; strong depth with foreground controlled pack detail, midground network geometry, background infrastructure.  
Lighting/mood: luminous cool daylight, precise, calm, credible, quietly ambitious; crisp detail with natural materials.  
Colour palette: neutral white, graphite, steel, restrained muted teal and a very small controlled crimson accent; avoid a blue-dominated look.  
Constraints: no people, no logos, no readable brand names, no medical claims, no NHS marks, no country flags, no text, no watermark, no syringes, no dramatic sci-fi holograms, no dirty or unsafe warehouse, no pills scattered on surfaces.  
Avoid: generic stock-lab aesthetic, hospital patient imagery, fake UI dashboards, cyberpunk neon, heavy blur, dark moody scene, excessive glass, AI collage artefacts.

## Supporting asset system

Supporting visuals use original code-native information design so relationships remain crisp at any density and do not simulate real products or facilities. The system includes:

- Oncology and specialty: connected scientific nodes without molecule or efficacy claims.
- Quality and batch integrity: controlled packs connected to evidence and release checkpoints.
- Digital traceability: batch, document, order and audit nodes in one network.
- Partnership pathway: qualification stages without partner logos.
- Six distinct Insights covers mapped to the actual article themes.

## Technical rules

- Hero source: 1672 × 941 PNG retained as the production master.
- Production JPEGs: 1672 × 941 and 1200 × 675, quality-controlled for responsive delivery.
- Information design: 1200 × 675 or larger viewBox, no external fonts, scripts or linked resources.
- All page images have intrinsic dimensions; non-critical assets are lazy-loaded.
- The official logo is never passed to an image generator and remains byte-identical to the supplied masters.
- No generated person or portrait may be presented as a NovaPharm executive.
