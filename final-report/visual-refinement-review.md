### Regulatory

- added a quality-control photographic introduction;

- retained all authorisation and regulatory-status caveats.

### Partners

- replaced the illustration-led pathway introduction with qualified scientific photography;
- rebuilt all ten partner segments as image-led cards;
- retained explicit language that no pictured organisation is a confirmed partner.

### Technology

- introduced a photographic technology-and-evidence story;
- strengthened the platform architecture presentation while preserving live, development and planned maturity labels;
- avoided presenting AI, blockchain or external integrations as deployed.

## Motion decision

A motion-enhanced still-image hero was selected rather than a video hero. This creates a premium effect with materially lower transfer cost and fewer autoplay, accessibility and mobile constraints. The design uses native CSS animation and minimal JavaScript only.

## Recommended review sequence

1. Homepage at 1440 × 900 and 390 × 844.
2. Motion pause/play and reduced-motion operating-system preference.
3. Seven-stage roadmap sequence on desktop and mobile.
4. Batch Integrity image, disclosure and text contrast.
5. Leadership index and the Vishal, Prabhakar and Girish profile crops.
6. Services gallery and all eight service sections.
7. Regulatory sticky image and control sequence.
8. All ten partner cards and the ten-stage partner journey.
9. Technology image story and maturity model.
10. Safari/WebKit and Chromium screenshots at the established acceptance matrix.

## Deliberate non-changes

- no Azure, DNS, domain, portal, database or SharePoint change;
- no regulatory-status escalation;
- no new claim of MHRA authorisation, product availability, NHS supply or facility ownership;
- no fabricated leadership portrait;
- no automatic merge or public release.
