4. **Responsibility architecture in outsourced oncology programmes**

5. **Assessing specialist medicines through product-readiness evidence**
   Original value: explain the six readiness dimensions and the difference between a commercial opportunity and evidence sufficient for an accountable gate. Do not name products or approval probability.

6. **Why oncology supply forecasting requires governed data**
   Original value: define the data, baseline, backtesting, override, bias and drift controls required before a forecasting pilot can influence planning. Explicitly reject the business plan's unverified stockout-reduction figure as a public claim.
