## Purpose

The business plan is a strategy and discovery source, not proof that a licence, product, partnership, customer relationship, financial result or technical capability exists. This register controls which themes may inform public content and which claims require independent evidence or must remain private.

## Evidence classes

| Class | Meaning | Public treatment |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A | Independently verifiable public fact | Publish only with a current authoritative source. |
| B | Board-approved strategy or intention | May be described as planned, in development, a target or subject to approval. |
| C | Claim requiring third-party evidence or written approval | Do not publish as fact until the evidence is recorded and approved. |
| D | Confidential or commercially sensitive information | Exclude from public pages and the public AI index. |
| E | Unsafe or misleading for the current public release | Prohibit unless the factual and regulatory position changes and formal review is completed. |

## Publication workflow

1. A proposed statement is linked to a claim-register identifier.
2. The owner records the evidence class, source, approval state, review date and re-verification date.
3. High-risk pharmaceutical, commercial and partnership statements receive a human claims review.
4. Generated public pages and the AI knowledge index are scanned for prohibited language.
5. AI may draft or flag contradictions; it cannot approve publication.

## Excluded source material

The private business plan is not copied into the public website, generated HTML, client JavaScript, sitemap, structured data or AI knowledge index. Financial forecasts, pricing, supplier details, proposed products, immigration material and other private annex content remain outside the public retrieval boundary.
