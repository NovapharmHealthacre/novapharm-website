## Evidence-gate result

The reviewed business plan supports pharmaceutical strategy, sourcing, quality/QMS/GDP planning, pharmacovigilance planning, formulation and analytical development, technology transfer, external development partnerships and clinical/regulatory programme experience among senior contributors. It does **not** evidence NovaPharm as an operational full-service CRO, a trial sponsor, an investigator-site network, a clinical laboratory, a patient-recruitment organisation, an in-house biometrics group or an IMP depot.

The candidate public proposition is therefore limited to programme coordination and evidence architecture. Specialist operational functions are described as qualification-dependent partner delivery. Sponsor duties remain explicit.
