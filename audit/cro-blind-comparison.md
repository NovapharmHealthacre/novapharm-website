# CRO Blind Comparison

- Candidate date: 18 July 2026
- Status: final internal expert comparison completed; no independent user panel is claimed

## Method

The final 1440-pixel candidate was reviewed against the identity-masked category set and scorecard already created for PR 11. Organisation names and logos were excluded from the decision criteria. The competitor set was not scraped or republished in this refinement; existing lawful research observations were reused.

The review compared first-viewport clarity, sponsor utility, responsibility transparency, evidence discipline, visual authority, mobile composition, accessibility and conversion quality. Global scale, trial volume and proprietary infrastructure were excluded because NovaPharm cannot evidence them.

The before/after candidate comparison is committed at `audit/evidence/cro-owner-review/before-after-comparison.jpg`. It records the actual previous and final generated pages rather than a recreated mock-up.

## Final Comparison

| Dimension | Common category pattern | Final NovaPharm candidate | Decision |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Proposition | Broad service taxonomy | One evidence-and-accountability proposition | NovaPharm is clearer for its narrower audience |
| Responsibility | Delivery commonly implied behind one brand | NovaPharm-led, qualified-specialist and sponsor-retained lanes | NovaPharm is more transparent |
| Programme orientation | Long capability menu | Eight-stage navigator with decision context | NovaPharm is more useful before commissioning |
| No-fit guidance | Rarely explicit | Conventional full-service CRO is recommended when infrastructure requires it | NovaPharm earns trust through candour |
| Visual system | Stock laboratory or corporate photography | Evidence-led hero, original information graphics and verified leadership portraits | NovaPharm is distinctive without asserting facilities |
| Human credibility | Client logos, metrics and case studies | Verified senior leadership only | Category leaders retain stronger proof of delivery scale |
| Page length | Long service catalogues | 1,478 visible words with progressive disclosure | Final candidate is materially easier to scan |
| Mobile | Desktop content stacked downward | Above-fold dual decision, two-column navigator and controlled section rhythm | Final candidate is intentionally composed |
| Claims risk | Breadth can imply infrastructure | Visible scope boundary and representative-media captions | NovaPharm has lower misinterpretation risk |

## Decision

The candidate does not and should not claim category leadership by operational scale. It is a category-leading candidate for **responsibility clarity, evidence architecture and UK pathway continuity** within its narrower coordination proposition.

Independent sponsor interviews remain the appropriate next validation before material commercial expansion. Search rank, conversion performance and market preference are not inferred from this internal expert review.
