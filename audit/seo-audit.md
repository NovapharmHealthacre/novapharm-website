# SEO Audit

## Issues Found
- Good initial metadata existed, but SEO was concentrated into a few `.html` pages.
- Missing clean page architecture for target commercial keywords.
- Missing structured breadcrumb strategy across all pages.
- Missing FAQ schema for generative and long-tail search.
- Missing dedicated pages for company profile, regulatory services, investor information, product portfolio, careers and distributor opportunities.
- Sitemap did not include the new enterprise content architecture.

## Implemented
- Clean canonical URLs for all required pages.
- Metadata, Open Graph, Twitter Cards and canonical tags on every generated page.
- JSON-LD Organization, WebPage, BreadcrumbList, Service and FAQ schema.
- XML sitemap regenerated.
- Robots rules updated to protect private portal/admin routes.
- Internal linking expanded through global nav and footer.
